RESIDENTIAL DESIGN

One popular residential structure model is the platform frame, which is commonly used in modern construction. This method involves constructing the floor and wall frames separately, with the floor frames serving as the platform for the walls. It allows for efficient building and flexibility in design, making it a favorite among builders and homeowners. Another widely utilized model is the balloon frame, which features continuous wall studs that extend from the foundation to the roof.
